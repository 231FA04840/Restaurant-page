About Us

Welcome to Amrutham, a culinary journey into the heart of South India. Our restaurant was founded with a passion for authentic flavors and a commitment to serving traditional recipes passed down through generations. We believe that food is not just sustenance, but an experience that brings people together.

Every dish at Amrutham is prepared with the freshest local ingredients and a touch of our unique home-style cooking. From the rich spices of Andhra to the subtle flavors of Kerala, our menu is a celebration of the diverse and vibrant cuisine of the region. We are more than a restaurant; we are a family dedicated to sharing our heritage with you.
