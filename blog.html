The Art of South Indian Breakfast

By Amrutham Team | August 1, 2025

South Indian breakfast, or "tiffin," is more than just a meal; it's a cultural staple. From the fluffy idlis and crispy dosas to the savory vadas and comforting upma, each dish tells a story of tradition and flavor. At Amrutham, we honor these time-tested recipes, preparing each item with authentic ingredients and a passion for taste.

We source our rice and lentils locally to ensure the highest quality. Our fermented batters are prepared in-house, giving our dosas and idlis that perfect texture and tangy flavor. Pair your tiffin with our rich, aromatic filter coffee for the quintessential South Indian experience.
